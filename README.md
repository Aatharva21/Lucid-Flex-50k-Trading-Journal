A lightweight **trading journal built specifically for traders using the Lucid Flex 50K funded account.**

Many trading journal platforms charge **monthly subscriptions** for features like trade tracking, analytics, and performance review.
Lucid Flex 50 provides a **free alternative with similar core functionality**, built for traders who want a simple and efficient way to review their trades.

---

# Why This Journal Exists

Traders using funded accounts like **Lucid Flex 50K** must maintain strict discipline and consistent performance.

However:

• Most journaling platforms require **paid subscriptions**
• Many tools are **overly complex for simple trade review**
• Some traders prefer a **private journal that runs locally**

Lucid Flex 50 solves this by providing a **clean, browser-based journal that works locally and costs nothing.**

---

# Local Data Storage

All journal data is stored **locally in your browser**.

This means:

• Your progress is **saved automatically on your device**
• No external servers or accounts are required
• Your trading data stays **private**

To continue tracking your progress, simply **open the journal using the same browser and the same email profile used previously**.

⚠️ Since the data is stored locally, clearing browser storage or switching browsers may remove saved progress.
